Who is the GOAT???

The Ronaldo Vs Messi Debate

This page is dedicated to the ongoing debeate in World Soccer over the last decade on who the best player of all time is. This conversation includes two great players: Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi. Both have won multiple titles and championships on their respective club teams. The one thing they both miss is the coveted World Cup. This year is expected to be both of their last attempts at the feat and with legacy on the line the trophy means that much more.
